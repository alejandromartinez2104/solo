HUNTER PROFILE

From E-Rank to Shadow Monarch

Once known as the "World's Weakest Hunter," Sung Jin Woo's life changed forever when he obtained the mysterious System. Through countless battles and trials, he evolved from an E-Rank hunter to the most powerful being on Earth.

As the Shadow Monarch, Jin Woo commands an army of shadow soldiers extracted from the souls of his defeated enemies. His power knows no bounds, and his influence extends across dimensions.

With the ability to level up infinitely and grow stronger with each battle, Jin Woo stands as humanity's greatest protector against the forces that threaten our world.
